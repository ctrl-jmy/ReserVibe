About ReserVibe

ReserVibe is a modern, pastel‑themed reservation platform designed to make booking effortless and delightful. Whether you're planning a dinner, a private party, a conference, or a spa day, ReserVibe gives you a clear view of daily availability (max 5 bookings per day) and allows you to manage your reservations in real time.

Built with simplicity and elegance in mind, our system features a live calendar, instant booking confirmations, secure file uploads, role‑based access (admin & regular users), and real‑time updates. We believe that planning should be as relaxing as the experience itself – that's why we created ReserVibe.
